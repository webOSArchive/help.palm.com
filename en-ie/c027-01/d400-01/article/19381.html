Use restricted dialling

Before You Begin

Get your PIN2 from your wireless service provider. If you enter your PIN2 incorrectly and your SIM card locks, get your PUK2 from your wireless service provider.

BYB Close

  1. Open ! Phone  > Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts > Restricted Dialling.
  2. To turn restricted dialling on or off: Tap On or Off > enter the PIN2 > Next.
  3. To edit the restricted number dialling list: With restricted dialling turned on, tap View Restricted Dialling List and do one of the following:
    • To add an entry: Add an entry > enter the PIN2 > enter the name and phone number > Done.
    • To edit an entry: Tap the entry > enter the PIN2 > edit the information > Done.
    • To delete an entry: Swipe the entry off the side of the screen > enter the PIN2 > Delete.

When restricted dialling is turned on, you can make calls to or answer calls from the numbers on your restricted number list only.
