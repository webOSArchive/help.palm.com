Change your voicemail greeting

Not all wireless service providers support the option to record a custom voicemail greeting. Check with your wireless service provider for information.

  1. Open ! Phone  > Menu (upper left) > Voicemail Greeting.
  2. Under Greeting Type, tap No greeting to use the default greeting from your wireless service provider. Tap Custom to use a greeting you record.
  3. If you selected Custom in step 2, use the onscreen controls to review your current custom greeting.
  4. Do one of the following:
    • To record a new greeting: Tap Record a new greeting. Use the onscreen controls to record your greeting.
    • To keep the new greeting: Tap .
    • To discard the greeting: Tap > Re-record > start recording again.
