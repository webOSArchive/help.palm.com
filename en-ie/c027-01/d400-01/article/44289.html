Use the Data Transfer Assistant (DTA)

The Data Transfer Assistant (DTA) on your smartphone completes a process that begins on your computer to export your contacts, calendar events, tasks, and memos/notes. To start the part of this export that happens on your computer, go to hpwebos.com/migrate-webOS to download the DTA and prepare your data for export to your smartphone.

We recommend that you use the DTA only once for each data source on your computer. If you use the DTA to transfer data from a data source more than once, you are very likely to get duplicates of your data.

The DTA is not a synchronisation application. Changes you make to data after it is on your smartphone do not show up in the desktop software. If you want to continue to synchronise with desktop software, go to hpwebos.com/sync-solutions to see if there are third-party solutions available for your smartphone. If there are no solutions available for your smartphone, check with the developers of existing sync software for other webOS devices to see if they are developing a solution for your smartphone.

The DTA allows you to select whether to transfer your data into one of your online accounts (such as Exchange or Google) or to your HP webOS Account. Not all of your data is necessarily transferred to the online account you select. The DTA tells you what data is transferred, and where, at the time you select the account. At that point, you can change your mind—but not afterwards.

 

  1. When you finish transferring data to your smartphone, disconnect the smartphone from your computer safely.
  2. On your smartphone, select the account you want to transfer your data to. Do one of the following:

    • If your account is listed: Tap it. If required, enter the account information > Sign In.
    • If you want to add an online account to your smartphone: Tap Add An Account > tap an account type > enter the account information > Sign In.

      To set up an account type that is not listed: Tap Find More > tap an app > follow the onscreen instructions to add the account.

      IMPORTANTIMPORTANT:

      If you decide to cancel or defer the transfer, you must do this before you complete the next step. Tap

      Menu (upper left) > Cancel Transfer > Cancel Transfer or Continue Later.

      To resume a transfer that you defer: Connect your smartphone to your computer using the USB cable > USB Drive. Then end the connection safely. Tap or add an account on the Data Transfer screen.

       

      Important

  3. Tap Finish Transfer > OK.

    NOTE: The import happens application by application and can take anywhere from a few minutes to one hour.

  4. When the notification that the data transfer is complete appears, tap it > Done.

What happens to my data after the transfer? If you transfer data to an online account, changes you make to the data on your smartphone are synchronised to the account on the server and vice versa. If you transfer data to your HP webOS Account, the data is stored on your smartphone and backed up to your HP webOS Account. This data is not available through any online account.

I have a local version of Outlook and Palm® Desktop on my PC. Can I transfer data from both? Yes, you can. If you have identical data stored in both locations, however, you are likely to see duplicate data on your smartphone.
