Share a web page using Touch to Share
  1. Open Web > open a web page that you want to send to your tablet.
  2. Tap the smartphone to the Centre button of the tablet.
  3. If Bluetooth was turned off on one or both devices, tap Turn on Bluetooth on one or both of the devices > touch the smartphone to the Centre button on the tablet again to complete the pair.

    The web page opens in full screen on the tablet.

You can also share a web page you open on an HP webOS tablet with your smartphone. Repeat steps 1 and 2 above, except open the web page on your tablet, then Touch to Share it on your smartphone.
