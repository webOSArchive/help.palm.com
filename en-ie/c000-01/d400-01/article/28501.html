5 Take out the battery

NOTE: Don’t try this method till you’ve already tried the four Ts before this. Because open applications are forced to close immediately during this kind of restart, some unsaved data may be lost.

  1. (Skip if not possible) Press and hold the power button. Tap Power > Shut Down/Swap Battery > Shut Down.
  2. Take out the battery, wait about 10 seconds and then reinsert it.

To learn how to remove the battery, go to kb.palm.com and enter this number:

  • Pre, Pre 2 & Pre Plus: 44919
  • Pre 3: [NEED KB NUMBER]
  • Pixi & Pixi Plus: 24398

 

Why this might help

Reinserting the battery performs a restart and also triggers an automatic clean-up of files that weren't closed properly.
