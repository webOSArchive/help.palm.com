Get help if a software update fails

During a wireless software update, there's a small chance that your smartphone may freeze and stop responding to taps or key presses. HP webOS Doctor restores your smartphone's functionality and updates it to the latest versions of the operating system and other system software. After you use the tool, backed-up data from your HP webOS Account is automatically restored to your smartphone.

You need the following to use the tool:

  • The USB cable that came with your smartphone
  • A desktop computer with the following:
    • Windows XP, Vista® or 7, or Mac OS 10.5
    • An available USB port (not through a hub or secondary USB connection)
    • The latest version of Java installed on your desktop computer (available from java.com)
    • 200 MB available storage space
  • An Internet connection capable of downloading large (10 MB-200 MB) files

To download the tool, go to hpwebos.com/support.
