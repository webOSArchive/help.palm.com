Start Exhibition

Do one of the following:

  • When the screen is off and the keyboard is closed, place your smartphone on an HP Touchstone. Exhibition starts automatically.
  • If you place your smartphone on a Touchstone while the screen is on, or when the screen is off but the keyboard is open, Exhibition starts when the smartphone has been inactive for the period defined in the Screen Lock settings. The screen does not lock.
  • Open Exhibition  > Start Exhibition. This starts Exhibition whether or not your smartphone is on a Touchstone.

Note: If you place your smartphone on a Touchstone when you are on a call or watching a video in the Videos or YouTube app, Exhibition does not start.
