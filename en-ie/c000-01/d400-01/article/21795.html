Set your international dialling method
  1. Open ! Phone  > Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts > Domestic Calls > Use.
  2. Tap one of the following:
    • To use your wireless service provider account for domestic calls: Tap Carrier.
    • To use your Skype account for domestic calls: Tap Skype.
    • To see a prompt asking you which method you want to use every time you make a domestic call: Tap Always Ask.

If you have a Skype account, you can set whether your smartphone dials domestic calls using your wireless service provider account or your Skype account. Alternatively, you can select to be prompted for the account to use each time you make a call.
