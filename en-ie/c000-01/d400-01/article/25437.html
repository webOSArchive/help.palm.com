Send contacts

Send all your contacts to a car kit (with Bluetooth) or send a contact (a vCard) with multimedia messaging (MMS) or email.

To send all contacts to a car kit, first ensure that your car kit is discoverable by your smartphone (learn how):

  1. Open Contacts  > Menu (upper left).
  2. Tap Send All To Car Kit.

To send one contact:

  1. Open Contacts  > tap a contact > Menu (upper left) > Send Contact.
  2. Tap one of the following:
  • Send To Car Kit: Your Bluetooth device needs to be on and detected.
  • Send Via MMS: Messaging opens.
  • Send Via Email: Email opens.
