Set an alarm

 Before You Begin

Make sure ringtone volume is on and you can hear it, and make sure the preference to play the alarm even when the ringer switch is off is set to Play Alarm (learn how). That way you can sleep through the night without being disturbed by phone calls, but your alarm still wakes you up in the morning.

BYB Close
  1. Open ! Clock .
  2. Tap (tap the screen if is not visible) > .
  3. Do the following:

    Alarm Preferences: Tap On/Off. Make sure the ringer switch and your Clock preference for playing an alarm are set to work the way you want.

    Name: Enter a name for the alarm.

    Occurs: Tap to select the alarm frequency.

    Time: Tap to set hour, minute and AM/PM for the alarm.

    Sound: Tap to open the list of ringtones > tap the name of a ringtone. Tap to preview it. If you have music on your smartphone, you can select a song as your alarm sound (learn how).

Use Clock to get up at the same time wherever you are; for example, the recurring 7am alarm you set in San Francisco rings at 7am in New York. Use Calendar to have your appointment times adjusted so that the notification for a 9am meeting in San Francisco will sound at midday when you're in New York.

When an alarm sounds on your smartphone, tap Dismiss to stop it or Snooze to have it sound again. The default snooze duration is 10 minutes, but you can change this to 5 minutes or 20 minutes (learn how).

NOTE: Alarms do not sound if you shut down your smartphone (learn how).
