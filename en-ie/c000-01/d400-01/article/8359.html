Work with images on a web page

The following options show or don't show depending on the size and position of the image or photo.

     
  • To open an image or photo in a new card: Press and hold Option  > tap the image > Open In New Card.
  • To share an image: Press and hold Option  > tap the image > Share Image. A new Email message opens with the image attached.
  • To copy an image or photo to Photos: Press and hold Option  > tap the image > Copy to Photos.
