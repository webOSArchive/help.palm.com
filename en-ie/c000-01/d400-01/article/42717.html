Use Flash content

When you open a web page, Flash content on the page appears with a play  button on a black background. Do either of the following:

  • To play the Flash content at its current size: Tap .
  • To play and zoom in on the Flash content: Double-tap it.

Keep the following in mind:

  • When you see in the bottom right-hand corner of the screen, you know you're working with Flash content and not the web page.
  • To use playback controls with Flash content and interact with other active areas: Tap the Flash content.
  • You cannot zoom in/out while working with Flash content.
  • To stop working with Flash content and return to the web page: Tap .

The ability to watch Flash content is turned on by default. To turn it off: Menu (upper left) > Preferences. In Enable Flash Content, tap On to switch it from On to Off.

To have Flash content begin playing as soon as you open a website, without your tapping the play button: Menu (upper left) > Preferences. In Autoload Flash Content, tap Off to switch it from Off to On.
