Insert the SIM card
  1. If the USB cable is attached to the smartphone, disconnect the USB cable.
  2. If a wired headset is attached to the smartphone, disconnect the headset.
  3. Shut down the smartphone (learn how).
  4. Slide out the keyboard.
  5. Firmly hold the middle of the smartphone with one hand. With the thumb of your other hand pressing on the HP logo at the back of the smartphone, place a fingernail in the notch below the keyboard, and pull the cover away from the body of the smartphone.
  6. To remove the battery: Grasp the tab and pull it up to lift the battery out of the compartment. IMPORTANTIMPORTANT:

    You must remove the battery first whenever you insert or remove the SIM card.

    Important
  7. Slide the SIM card into the rectangular slot, with the gold contacts facing down.
  8. Replace the battery.
  9. To replace the cover, first fit the cover over the buttons on the top of the smartphone, and then press all clasps to fasten the cover to the body of the smartphone.
  10. Press and hold the power button on the top right-hand corner of your smartphone until you see a logo on the screen (approximately five seconds).

If you inserted or removed the SIM card before you removed the battery, your smartphone cannot connect to the network. To restore the network connection, do the following:

  1. Remove the battery.
  2. Remove the SIM card.
  3. Insert the SIM card and replace the battery.
  4. Make sure your phone on your smartphone is not in aeroplane mode (learn how).
