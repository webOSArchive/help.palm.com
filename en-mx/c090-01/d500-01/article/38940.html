Create and send an email message
  1. Open  Email .
  2. If you have not set up an email account, you need to do so first (learn how).
  3. Tap at the botttom of the message list panel.
  4. (Optional) Tap From to change the email account you are using to send the message. This option appears only if you have more than one email account.
  5. Tap the To field, and do one of the following:
    • Enter a contact name, initials, or email address > tap the address.
    • Tap  to see your contacts > tap the contact you want or enter a name or address > tap the address.
    • Enter the full email address for a recipient who is not a contact.
  6. (Optional) Tap To to open the Cc and Bcc fields > tap any of the fields and enter an address.
  7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 to enter additional recipients.

    If you are adding multiple recipients and you want to edit one, tap the email address you want to change and enter the new address.

  8. Tap the Subject field > enter the subject > Enter > enter the body text.
  9. (Optional) To set the priority for the message: Tap > tap a priority level.
  10. (Optional) To discard a message: Tap > Discard Message.
  11. Tap Send.

If you have set up an email account, you can also start writing an email from Card view. Tap Just type, and start typing a message. Scroll down the list of search options, and tap New Email. The Email application opens at a new email message, with the text you entered in the body of the message.
