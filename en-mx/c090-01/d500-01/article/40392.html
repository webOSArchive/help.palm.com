Enter symbols and alternate characters

Do one of the following:

  • To enter a symbol that appears on a number key: Tap Shift  > the key. You don't need to hold Shift while tapping the number key.
  • To enter a symbol from the symbol keyboard: Tap to display the symbol keyboard > a symbol key. Tap to return to the alphanumeric keyboard.
  • To enter other symbols or alternate keyboard characters: Tap and hold any letter or number key to see the symbols or characters associated with that key > a symbol.

Not all keys contain alternate characters or symbols. When you tap and hold a key, you see ... at the bottom of most keys. The ... means that alternate characters/symbols are available for those keys. If a key does not display ..., it does not have any alternate characters or symbols. Likewise, the keys on the symbol keyboard do not contain alternate characters or symbols.
