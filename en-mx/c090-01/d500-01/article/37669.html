Use Calendar for the first time
  1. Open  Calendar .
  2. Do one of the following:
    • To set up an online account to sync: To set up an account that is listed, tap the account type, and go to step 3. To set up an account type that is not listed, tap Find More to find an app and follow the instructions onscreen.
    • To use accounts you have set up already in another app: Tap Go. This opens your HP webOS Account and any online accounts, and you can start creating events in these accounts.
    • To use Calendar without creating an online account: Tap your HP webOS Account name. You can now start creating events in your HP webOS Account.
  3. Enter the account information > tap Sign in.
  4. If you are setting up an Exchange account, to keep your Exchange account secure, you must enter a password or PIN that is required to unlock the device. Tap Set Password or Set Pin > enter a password or PIN > Done.
  5. Depending on the account, you may be presented with options for which apps should download data from that account. For each app, tap On or Off > Create Account.
