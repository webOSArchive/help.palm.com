Install HP Play

On a Windows computer:

  1. Open your computer's browser and go to hpplay.com.
  2. Click Download Now.
  3. Check the box to agree to the terms and conditions > Download.
  4. If your Internet security is blocking the site from downloading files to your computer, click the notification at the top of the screen Download File.
  5. Click Run.
  6. If an "Unknown publisher" message appears, click Run to continue.
  7. Click Next.
  8. (Optional) Uncheck Launch HP Play to stop HP Play from opening immediately.
  9. Click Finish.

On a Mac:

  1. Open your computer's browser and go to hpplay.com.
  2. Click Download HP Play Beta for Mac.
  3. Click Save File > Save.
  4. When HP Play has finished downloading, double-click the HP Play .dmg file in Downloads.
  5. Drag the HP Play icon to the Applications folder.
