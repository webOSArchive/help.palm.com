Start Exhibition

Do one of the following:

  • When the screen is off, place your device on an HP Touchstone. Exhibition starts automatically.
  • If you place your device on a Touchstone while the screen is on, Exhibition starts when the device has been inactive for the period defined in the Screen Lock settings. The screen does not lock.
  • Open  Exhibition  > Start Exhibition. This starts Exhibition whether or not your device is on a Touchstone.

Note: If you place your device on a Touchstone when you are on a call or watching a video in the Pictures & Videos or YouTube app, Exhibition does not start.
