Turn all wireless services on/off (airplane mode)

Do one of the following:

  • Open the system menu > Turn on/off Airplane Mode.
  • Press and hold  the power button > Airplane Mode or Turn off Airplane Mode.

In airplane mode, you can turn the Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or VPN features on individually: Open the system menu > Bluetooth > Turn on Bluetooth, Wi-Fi > Turn on Wi-Fi, or VPN.
