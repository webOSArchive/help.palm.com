Browse applications in HP webOS App Catalog
  1. Open  HP App Catalog .
  2. If prompted, select your country.
  3. If prompted, review the terms of use > Accept.
  4. Do one of the following:
    • To browse Pivot, a magazine showcasing new apps: Tap Featured at the bottom of the screen. Swipe right and left on the screen to turn pages.
    • To search by category: Tap Categories at the bottom of the screen > a category. To view subcategories for a category, tap All [category name] at the top of the screen > a subcategory. When the subcategory name appears at the top of the screen, tap it to return to the list of subcategories..
    • To search applications you bookmarked: Tap Bookmarks at the bottom of the screen.
    • To search by name: Tap Search at the bottom of the screen > type a search term > . A list of apps whose name or category matches your search term appears.
    • To search within featured, paid, free, or new apps only: After you start a category, bookmark, or name search, tap Top, Paid, Free, or New at the top of the screen.
  5. Tap an app name to display app details.
  6. On the app details screen, do any of the following:
    • To see a video of the app: Tap a thumbnail image that displays the icon. Not all apps include videos.
    • To see a larger view of a thumbnail image, if available: Tap a thumbnail image that does not display the icon.
    • To see more thumbnails: Swipe right or left on the thumbnail area of the screen.
    • To bookmark the app: Tap at the top of the screen.You can also bookmark an app in the app search results list by tapping to the right of the app name.
    • To read reviews: Under Reviews, tap Positive or Negative. Scroll to see more positive or negative reviews.
    • To share info about the app with a friend: Tap Share, and then tap Email or Text Message. Sharing via text message is available only if your device is connected to an HP webOS smartphone (learn how).
    • To see more apps from this app developer: Tap Developer Apps.
    • To go to the developer's product support page: Tap Support (if available).
    • To report a problem with the app: Tap Report a Problem. Tap Bug > the type of problem you are reporting > Tap here to begin typing > enter the problem description > Send.
