Call or chat with a Skype contact
  1. Open  Contacts .
  2. Tap a contact > tap the contact name again.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • To start an IM chat with a buddy: Tap Chat > enter your message > Enter.
    • To make a voice call to a buddy: Tap Voice Call.
    • To make a video call to a buddy: Tap Video Call.

When you add or a create a Skype account on your device, your Skype buddies are added to Contacts. From here, you can chat with a buddy, or make a voice or video call to a buddy.
