Receive and read email messages
  1. Open  Email .
  2. If the folders for the account you want are hidden, tap the account name in the left-hand panel to display the folders.
  3. Tap the folder you want to check messages for. If synchronization doesn't start, tap .
  4. Tap a message in the middle panel to open it.
  5. (Optional) To expand a message to full-screen view, tap on the right-hand panel.
  6. (Optional) Do any of the following:
    • To call a number: Tap the number > . To make phone calls, your device must be connected to a webOS smartphone (learn how).
    • To send an email to an email address: Tap the email address > enter your message > Send.
    • To go to a web address: Tap it.
    • To copy the body of an email so you can paste it into a memo or other document: Tap the message text > Menu (upper left) > Edit > Select All. Tap the selected text > tap Copy.

The Email application synchronizes messages anytime you open a mail folder. It also synchronizes messages on a schedule that you can set (learn how). To sync messages when you have a folder already open: Tap .
