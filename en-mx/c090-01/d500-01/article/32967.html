Send and receive IM messages

Before You Begin

To send instant messages, your device must be connected to a Wi-Fi network. BYB Close

  1. Open Messaging .
  2. Do one of the following to start or continue a conversation:
    • Tap to see your buddies > tap a buddy name. If there's more than one account for that buddy, the most recently used IM account is opened. To switch to another IM account, tap the IM account name in the upper-right > tap an IM address.
    • Tap to see your favorites > tap a favorite.
    • Tap to see your conversations > tap a conversation.
    • Tap > New Conversation > To > enter an IM address > Enter, or tap to search for a contact.
  3. Tap Enter message here > enter the message text > Enter. You can use emoticons in your message (learn how).
  4. When the message recipient replies, the reply appears below your message in the conversation. Repeat step 3 to send another message.
  5. (Optional) Do any of the following in a message you receive:
    • To call a number: Tap the number > . This feature is available only if your device is connected to a compatible HP webOS smartphone or you have a video calling account set up on your device (learn how).
    • To add a number to Contacts: Tap the number > Add to Contacts.
    • To send an email: Tap the email address.
    • To go to a web address: Tap it.
