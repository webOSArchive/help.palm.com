Show or hide folders in the Accounts List panel
  1. Open  Email .
  2. Tap to the right of Favorites or tap an account name > tap the folder name you want to open.
  3. (Optional) Menu (upper left) > About This Folder to view information such as last sync and number of messages.
  4. Tap to hide the folders under Favorites or an account name.

The Accounts List panel contains expandable folders for Favorites and each of your email accounts. The Favorites folder gives you quick access to the inbox for each or your accounts. By default, it also contains a folder called All Inboxes, which contains a merged view of all your inboxes. You can also set a preference to add a folder called All Flagged under Favorites to display all your flagged messages in a single folder.
