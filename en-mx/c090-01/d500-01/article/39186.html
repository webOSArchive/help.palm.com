Get directions
  1. Open Maps .
  2. If prompted, tap Allow Once.
  3. Tap .
  4. If required, tap OK to turn on your location.
  5. If you want to start at a location other than your current location, tap Current Location and enter a new starting location, or tap a location you viewed recently.
  6. Tap End > enter your destination > tap Enter. To get directions to an address you saved, tap > Bookmarks or Recents > tap a location > Directions To Here > Go.
  7. Do any of the following:
    • For driving directions: Tap .
    • For public transport directions: Tap .
    • For walking directions: Tap .
  8. Do one of the following:
    • To see step-by-step directions on the map: Tap the dimmed green flag that marks your starting location > tap the next step, and so on. To retrace your route: Swipe from left to right along the steps.
    • To see directions as a list of numbered steps: Tap . To close the directions list: Tap .
Note

NOTE: The Maps app cannot determine your location and therefore cannot work properly unless a location service is turned on (learn how).
