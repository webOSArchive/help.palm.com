Turn new email notifications on/off

The settings you select here apply to individual email accounts. You can apply different settings to each email account.

  1. Open  Email  > Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts > tap an account in Accounts.
  2. Tap Show Icon > On or Off.
  3. Tap Alert > tap any of the following:

    Vibrate: The smartphone vibrates with no other sound.

    System Sound: The system sound plays. If the ringer is off, the smartphone vibrates.

    Ringtone > Ringtone: Tap a ringtone. A ringtone that you choose plays.

    Mute: No sound plays and the smartphone does not vibrate.
