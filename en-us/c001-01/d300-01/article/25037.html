Add or change a contact photo
  1. While creating (learn how) or editing (learn how) a contact, do one of the following:
    • To add or change the photo in the default account: Tap .
    • In a linked contact, to add or change the photo of another account: Tap the account button in the upper-right corner > tap the other account.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • To take a new photo: Tap New Photo > take the picture > Assign to Contact.
    • To use an existing photo: Tap the album containing the photo > tap the photo > Assign to Contact.
