Use voice dialing when the screen is locked
  1. Open Screen & Lock  .
  2. Under Voice Dialing, tap On or Off next to Enable When Locked.

If you turn on the preference to use voice dialing when the screen is locked, you access voice dialing by turning on the screen if it is off (learn how), and then pressing and holding volume. You do not need to enter your PIN or password to unlock the screen.

If this preference is on and you have a headset attached to your smartphone, you can access voice dialing when the screen is locked by pressing and holding the button on the headset--you donâ€™t need to turn the screen on first.
