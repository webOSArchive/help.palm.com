Shut down your smartphone

Do one of the following:

  • Press and hold the power button > Power > Shut Down/Swap Battery > Shut Down.
  • Open Device Info  > Reset Options > Shut Down > Shut Down.

Shutting down your smartphone turns both the screen and all wireless services off, and puts your smartphone into deep sleep. You can shut down your smartphone when you are not going to use it for an extended period of time.

To turn both the screen and wireless services back on, press and hold the power button until a logo appears onscreen.
