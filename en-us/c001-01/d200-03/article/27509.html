Line up your favorite applications in Quick Launch
  1. Open the Launcher (learn how).
  2. Tap and hold a Quick Launch icon > wait for a visual cue > drag the icon onto the Launcher.
  3. Tap and hold a Launcher icon > wait for a visual cue > drag the icon onto the Quick Launch bar.

You must remove an application from Quick Launch before you can add another one. You can remove any application except Launcher.
