Update downloaded applications
  1. Open App Catalog  >  .

    2. The list of your downloaded apps appears. Apps with an available update display an icon to the right of the app name with a number and a green arrow.

  2. Do one of the following:
    • To update one app: Tap the icon to the right of the app name.
    • To update all apps: Tap Install Updates.
