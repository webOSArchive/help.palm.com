View your friends' Facebook page
  1. Open  Facebook  > .
  2. Do any of the following:
    • To invite someone to be your friend on Facebook: Tap Search > All Users > enter the name of the person > tap the name > Add As Friend.
    • To search for a friend: Tap Search > enter the name of the person > tap the name.
    • To view a friend’s Facebook page: Tap Search > enter the name of the person > tap the name. At the bottom of the screen, tap Wall for postings, tap Info for your friend’s info, and Pics for photo albums.
    • To post to a friend's wall: Tap Search > enter the name of the person > tap the name. Tap Write something > enter a comment > Post.
    • To keep track of friends’ birthdays: Tap Events > Birthdays.
    • To view pages your friends or contacts have created: Tap Search > Pages.
