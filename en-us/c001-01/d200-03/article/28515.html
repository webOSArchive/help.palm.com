6 Connect your phone to your computer and reinstall webOS software

NOTE: Don’t try this method till you’ve already tried the five Ts before this.

  1. Remove the battery.

    To learn how to remove the battery, go to kb.palm.com and enter this number:

    • Pre, Pre 2, & Pre Plus: 44919
    • Pixi & Pixi Plus: 24398
  2. Write down the serial number (S/N) printed in the battery compartment.
  3. Open your computer’s web browser and go to palm.com/rom.
  4. Click the link to run webOS Doctor, enter the serial number, and follow the instructions.

Why this might help

Something may have happened to your phone that corrupted its operating system. Installing the latest version of the webOS platform refreshes all memory.

Want help while you use the webOS Doctor?

Go to palm.com/support. Select your phone and wireless service provider, and click Support Services. Select one of the Palm support options.
