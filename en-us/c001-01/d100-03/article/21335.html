Create and send an email message
  1. Open Email .
  2. If this is the first time you have opened Email and you have set up at least one email account, tap Done. If you have not set up an email account, you need to do so first (learn how).
  3. Tap .
  4. (Optional) Tap From to change the email account you are using to send the message. This option appears only if you have more than one email account.
  5. In the To field, do one of the following:
    • Enter the first or last name or initials > tap the address.
    • Tap  to see your contacts > enter the first or last name or initials > tap the address.
    • Enter the full email address for a recipient who is not a contact.
  6. (Optional) Tap To to open the Cc and Bcc fields > enter the address.
  7. Tap the To field and repeat steps 5 and 6 to enter additional recipients.
  8. Enter the subject >  > enter the body text.
  9. (Optional) To set the priority for the message: Menu (upper left) > Set As Normal priority or Set As High priority.
  10. (Optional) To discard a message: Menu (upper left) > Discard Message.
  11. Tap .
