Set up email when automatic setup fails

Follow these steps only if automatic setup (learn how) does not work for your email account.

Before You Begin

Get this info from your email provider or system administrator:

  • Account type (POP or IMAP)
  • Incoming and outgoing mail server names
  • Incoming mail server username and password
  • Incoming and outgoing server port numbers
  • Any SSL requirements for incoming and/or outgoing mail
  • Any authentication (ESMTP) requirements for outgoing mail
  • Root folder (IMAP acccounts only)

BYB Close

  1. Open Email .
  2. If this is the first email account you are setting up: Enter the email address and password.
  3. Menu (upper left) > Manual Setup.
  4. Tap the Mail Type field > tap your account type.
  5. Enter info for the incoming and outgoing mail server as needed, based on the info you learned.
  6. Tap Sign In.

Any other time you open Email, do this:

  1. Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts > Add An Account.
  2. Enter the email address and password.
  3. Menu (upper left) > Manual Setup.
  4. Tap the Mail Type field > tap your account type.
  5. Enter info for the incoming and outgoing mail server as needed, based on the info you learned.
  6. Tap Sign In.
