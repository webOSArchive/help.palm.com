Why this might help

Restarting your phone closes all applications, frees up all memory, and reinitializes all your wireless services (phone and Bluetooth® technology). A fresh start.

An occasional restart can be good for your phone just the way a restart can be good for your computer. Every once in a while, restart your phone.
