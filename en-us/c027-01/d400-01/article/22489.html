Enter advanced account settings

These preferences apply only to the account you select in step 2.

  1. Open  Email .
  2. Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts > tap an account.
  3. Set any of the following:
    • Account Name: The name that appears in the account list.
    • Full Name: The name you want to appear in the From field for messages you send.
    • Show Icon: Set whether a notification icon appears onscreen when a new message arrives.
    • Alert: When Show Icon is on, set whether a sound plays when new messages arrive (learn how).
    • Signature: Tap to create a signature that's added to outgoing messages (learn how).
    • Reply-to Address: A different email address for recipients to see and reply to.
    • Show Email: How many days' worth of messages to retrieve from the server.
    • Get Email: How frequently to synchronize email for this account.
    • Sync deleted emails: For POP accounts only, delete messages on the server when you delete them on your smartphone.
    • Sync server to device: For POP accounts only, delete messages on your smartphone when they are deleted on the server.
    • Default Folders: For IMAP accounts only, specify folders to hold your sent, draft, and deleted messages.
    • Change Login Settings: Learn how.
    • Remove Account: Learn how.
