Search for a business
  1. Open Maps .
  2. If prompted, tap Allow Once.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • To search for a business: Tap Search or Address > enter a business name or category > Enter . The closest match is shown with an orange marker and the number 1. Other locations that match your search are shown with a numbered blue marker. The number ranks the locations's proximity. To see your search results in a list, tap at the bottom of the screen. Tap beside a business name to see business details.
    • To find a business you saved: Tap > Bookmarks > tap a business.
    • To find an address you searched for recently: Tap > Recents > tap a business.
  4. (Optional) To save a business location: Tap its marker or tap the address at the bottom of the screen > > Save.
  5. (Optional) To get directions to a business from your current location: Tap its marker or tap the address at the bottom of the screen > > Go.
