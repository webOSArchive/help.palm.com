Delete an event
  1. Open  Calendar .
  2. Delete an event in one of two ways:
    • In Day view, press and hold Option   and tap an event. You have the option to delete just this event or the entire series, or to cancel the delete.
    • Tap the event name > Menu (upper left) > Delete Event > Delete. If this event is one of a series of repeating events, tap the option to delete just this event or the entire series.
    If you delete an event that is assigned to an online account, the event is deleted in the online account also.


    IMPORTANTIMPORTANT:

    When you delete a Microsoft Exchange event on your smartphone, notification is not sent to attendees.
