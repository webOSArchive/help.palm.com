Receive and read email messages
  1. Open Email .
  2. Tap to display the account folders > tap a folder.
  3. Tap a message to open it.
  4. (Optional) Do any of the following:
    • To call a number: Tap the number > .
    • To send a text message: Tap and hold the number > Text.
    • To send an email to an email address: Tap the email address > enter your message > .
    • To go to a web address: Tap it.
    • To share a link to a web site: Press and hold Option  > tap the link > Share Link.
    • To copy a link to a web site: Press and hold Option  > tap the link > Copy URL.
  5. Tap or  to view the previous or next message.

Email synchronizes messages anytime you open a mail folder. It also synchronizes messages on a schedule that you can set (learn how). To sync messages when you have a folder already open: Tap .
