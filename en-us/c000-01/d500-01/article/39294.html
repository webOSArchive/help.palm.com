Pair with an HP webOS smartphone using Touch to Share

Before You Begin

Your device must be connected to a Wi-Fi network to use Touch to Share.

BYB Close

  1. Make sure Bluetooth is turned on on your device (learn how) and on your smartphone (see the documentation that came with your smartphone).
  2. Touch the smartphone to your device, with the HP logo on the back of the smartphone touching the area of the Center button on your device.

    A ripple is displayed on the device screen to indicate that the smartphone has been placed correctly.

    A notification appears on both devices to indicate that the pairing was successful.

    The devices remain paired while in range (approximately 30 feet (10 meters)). If the devices move out of range of each other, provided the relationship between the two devices has not been deleted, they reconnect automatically upon receiving a phone call or text message when they are back within range.

    When you set up a Touch to Share relationship with a webOS smartphone, you can manage the connection preferences from the Bluetooth app (learn how).
