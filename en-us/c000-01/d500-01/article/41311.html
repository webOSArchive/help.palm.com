Use HP Play to sync music from your computer to your device

If you have music in your HP Play library on your computer, you can sync the songs in your HP Play library to the Music app on your device. If you have music in an iTunes library, the contents of that library are copied to HP Play when you install it on your computer (learn how), and you can then sync all your iTunes music to your device from HP Play. Music you add to iTunes after you install HP Play is copied to HP Play automatically.

You can set up HP Play to sync automatically with your device each time you connect it to your computer (learn how), or, if you choose not to sync automatically, you can manually sync HP Play with your device at any time (learn how). Playlists sync one-way, from HP Play on your computer to your device, and playlists synced from HP Play cannot be changed on your device.
