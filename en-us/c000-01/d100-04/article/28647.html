Browse App Catalog
  1. Open App Catalog .
  2. Review the terms of use, and tap Accept.
  3. Tap the icon of a featured application, or do one of the following:
    • To search by name: Type a search term > Enter .
    • To search apps by category: Tap the Browse Categories header, and then tap a category. If the header continues to appear, tap it to select subcategories.
    • To narrow your search by filtering: Tap an icon at the bottom of the screen.

    • To search within featured, top paid, top free, or new apps only: First tap the corresponding icon at the bottom of the screen, and then enter a search term.
  4. Tap an app name to display app details.
  5. On the app details screen, do any of the following:
    • To see a larger view: Tap a screenshot.
    • To read reviews: Tap Reviews.
    • To share info about this app with a friend: Tap Share > Email or Text Message.
    • To go to the developer's home page: Tap Developer Home.
    • To go to the developer's product support page: Tap Support (if available).
    • To view a video of the app in your phone's YouTube application: Tap the YouTube link (if available).
