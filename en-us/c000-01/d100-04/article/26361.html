Accept a pairing request from another device
  1. Open  Bluetooth .
  2. Make sure Bluetooth is on (learn how).
  3. Initiate pairing from the other device.
  4. When the connection request appears on your smartphone, tap Yes, Allow.
  5. If a passkey is not required, pairing is complete when the Devices list appears. If a passkey screen appears, there are three possible scenarios:
    • An automatically generated passkey appears on the passkey screen: Check the other device screen to verify that the passkey is correct. Tap Yes, connect to connect.
    • The screen displays a passkey generated by your smartphone: Enter the displayed passkey on the other device.
    • The screen prompts you to enter a passkey: Check the documentation that came with the other device to see if there's a required passkey. (If so, enter that passkey on your smartphone. If not, make up a passkey and enter it on both devices.)

To establish the relationship between your smartphone and another device, the Bluetooth app must be open, the app window must be maximized, and Bluetooth must be turned on.
