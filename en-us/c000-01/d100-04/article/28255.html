Use the keyboard

  1. Option: Press to enter numbers, punctuation, and symbols that appear above the letters on the keys, or to move the cursor. Press twice to turn on Option Lock.
  2. Shift
  3. Space
  4. Backspace
  5. Enter: Press to enter a line return, to move from field to field, or to accept the information you entered.
  6. Sym: Press to select characters like currency symbols and accented characters.
