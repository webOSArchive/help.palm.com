Update your HP webOS Account settings
  1. Open Backup  > Menu (upper left) > HP WebOS Account.
  2. Enter your HP webOS Account password > Sign In.
  3. Edit any of the following:

    First Name/Last Name: Change the first name and/or last name associated with your HP webOS Account.

    Email: Change the email address associated with your HP webOS Account. A verification email is sent to the new address. Click the link in the email to verify the new address.

    Security Question/Answer: Tap the current question > tap a new one > Answer > enter an answer.

    Change Password: Tap Change Password > enter your new password > confirm new password > Done.

 

IMPORTANTIMPORTANT:

To ensure that the data on your smartphone is consistent with that stored on your HP webOS Account, we recommend that you perform a manual backup (learn how) of your data both before and after you change any of your HP webOS Account settings.
