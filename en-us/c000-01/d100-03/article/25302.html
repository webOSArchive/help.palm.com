Flag location information for Facebook
  1. Open  Facebook  > .
  2. If prompted, tap Allow Once to turn on Location Services.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • To create a new location: Tap > enter a name for the location > (optional) enter a description > Create Place.
    • To select an existing location: Enter the location you want to check in to and tap it, or tap a location in the list of nearby locations.
  4. Menu (upper left) > Location.
  5. Tap any of the following:

    Flag Incorrect Info: Identify information that is incorrect.

    Flag Abusive: Identify information that is abusive.

    Flag Permanently Closed: Notify Facebook that a location is permanently closed.

    Flag Duplicate: Identify a duplicate item in the list of nearby locations.

While reviewing locations in Facebook Places, you might encounter information that you want to flag to Facebook.
