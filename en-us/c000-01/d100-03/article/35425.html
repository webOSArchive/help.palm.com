Get help if a software update fails

During a wireless software update, there’s a small chance that your phone may freeze and stop responding to taps or key presses. Palm® webOS Doctor™ restores your phone’s functionality and updates it to the latest versions of the operating system and other system software. After you use the tool, backed-up data from your Palm profile is automatically restored to your phone.

You need the following to use the tool:

  • The USB cable that came with your phone
  • A desktop computer with the following:
    • Windows XP, Vista®, or 7, or Mac OS 10.5
    • An available USB port (not through a hub or secondary USB connection)
    • The latest version of Java installed on your desktop computer (available from java.com)
    • 200MB available storage space
  • An Internet connection capable of downloading large (10MB-200MB) files

To download the tool, go to palm.com/support.
