Go to a website
  1. Do one of the following:

    • In the Launcher or Card view, type the site address (for example, hp.com) > tap Go to website.
    • Open Web  > enter the site address. If the page appears in the list of viewed pages, tap it to go there again. If not, press Enter .

      When entering a website address (URL), you don't need to enter "http://www."

  2. To cancel a page while it is loading, tap .
