The Music app organizes the audio content you add to your device under two main headings: Library and Playlists. The Library heading organizes your audio content into four predefined categories: Songs, Artists, Albums, and Genres. Tap a category to see content organized in that category. The Playlists list contains playlists that you create on the device and playlists you import from other sources, for example from HP Play.

By default, items in a Library category or playlist are displayed in a list in the right-hand panel. To see Album Art instead of the list, tap . To return to the list, tap .
