Dial from another application
  1. If a phone number appears in an application as an underlined link, tap the number. This displays the dial pad with the number already entered.
  2. Tap  to dial.
  3. If prompted, tap Bluetooth to make the call using a connected smartphone.

If a phone number appears in another application as an underlined link, you can begin dialing the number directly from that application. For example, you can dial phone numbers that appear in web pages; in messages; or in notes to calendar events.

Other apps that offer special ways to dial include Calendar, Email, and Memos.
