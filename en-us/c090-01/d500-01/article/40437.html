5. Tether your device to your computer and reinstall webOS

NOTE: Don't try this method till you've already tried the four Ts before this.

  1. On your computer's web browser, go to hpwebos.com/rom, sign in, and write down the device's product number and serial number.
  2. Go to hpwebos.com/rom and click the link to get webOS Doctor.
  3. Enter the device's product number and serial number and follow the instructions.

Why this might help

Installing the latest version of webOS wipes and refreshes all memory. Your preferences, installed software, and files in USB drive storage are not affected.

Want help while you use the webOS Doctor?

Go to hpwebos.com/support. Select your device and click Support Services. Select one of the support options.
