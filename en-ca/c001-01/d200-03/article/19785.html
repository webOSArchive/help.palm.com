Dial your national emergency number

To dial your national emergency number (such as 911, 112, or 999), do the following:

  1. Press power to wake up the screen if needed.
  2. Drag up to unlock the screen if needed.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • If Secure Unlock is not on (learn how), tap the center of the gesture area to display Card view if Card view is not displayed. Enter the emergency number, and tap the number to dial. You don’t have to press Option to enter numbers.
    • If Secure Unlock is on, tap Tap to dial emergency call, and then tap Emergency call. Edit the displayed number if needed, and then tap to dial.

If the phone is turned off (that is, if it’s in airplane mode), you don’t have to turn it on. It automatically tries to connect to a network to make the call.
