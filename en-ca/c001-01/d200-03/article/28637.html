Browse App Catalog
  1. Open App Catalog .
  2. Review the terms of use, and tap Accept.
  3. Swipe right or left on the thumbnails to see featured applications, or do one of the following:
    • To search by name: Tap Search > enter the search term > Enter .
    • To search apps by category: Tap Categories. Tap to see a list of categories. Tap to see a tag cloud. 
    • To view the most recently added apps: Tap What's New.
    • To view the most popular apps: Tap Hottest Apps.
  4. To sort the list of applications or to browse results by date, alphabetical order, rating, or price, tap a sort icon at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Tap an app name to display app details.
  6. On the app details screen, do any of the following:
    • To see a larger view: Tap a screenshot.
    • To read reviews: Tap Reviews.
    • To send a link to this application to a friend: Tap Share > Email or Text Message.
    • To go to the developer's home page: Tap Developer Home.
    • To go to the developer's product support page: Tap Support (if available).
