6 Tether your smartphone to your computer and reinstall webOS software

NOTE: Don’t try this method till you’ve already tried the five Ts before this.

  1. Open your computer’s web browser > go to hpwebos.com/account > write down your smartphone's serial number.
  2. Go to hpwebos.com/rom.
  3. Click the link to getwebOS Doctor > enter the serial number > follow the instructions.

Why this might help

Installing the latest version of webOS wipes and refreshes all memory. Your preferences and software are not affected.

Want help while you use the webOS Doctor?

Go to hpwebos.com/support. Select your smartphone and wireless service provider, and click Support Services. Select one of the support options.
