Open the connection menu
  1. Do one of the following:
    • Drag down from the upper-right corner of the smartphone onto the screen.
    • Tap the upper-right corner of the screen, where you see the battery and signal-strength icons.
  2. Tap a menu item to turn that wireless feature on or to display a list of menu items for that feature.

The connection menu lets you manage wireless services--the phone, BluetoothÂ® wireless technology, Wi-Fi, and VPN.

A downward-pointing arrow at the bottom of the menu means that more items are available. Scroll the menu to see more items.
