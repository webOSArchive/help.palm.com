Create an event
  1. Open Calendar .
  2. Tap All or an online account name in the upper-right corner > tap the calendar you want to use for this event.
    If you create an event in All Calendars view, the event is created in the calendar set as the default (learn how).
  3. Go to the day you want (learn how) > tap a blank time slot > enter the event name > press Enter .
    Don't look for a save button—your info is automatically saved when you make the back gesture to close the event.

In Day view, available time slots between events are compressed and labeled X Hrs Free. To expand free time so you can enter an event, tap X Hrs Free.

If you add an event to one of your online accounts, the info is also synchronized to your provider's server on the web. If you add the event to your Palm profile account, the info is saved and backed up to your Palm profile.
