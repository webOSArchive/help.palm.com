Choose how to copy media and other files

You have several options for copying media and other types of files from your computer to your phone:

  • You can drag and drop any media files to your phone using USB Drive mode (learn how).
  • You can download a third-party solution to facilitate the transfer of media files and personal data (contacts, calendar events, and so on) to your phone. Go to palm.com/sync-solutions to learn more.

NOTE: The webOS platform no longer supports Media Sync mode for copying files. Use one of the other options listed here to copy media files. Any files you copied using media sync are still available on your phone.
