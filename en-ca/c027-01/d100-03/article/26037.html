Customize event notification sounds
  1. Open  Calendar  > Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts.
  2. Tap the Event Reminders field > tap any of the following:


    Vibrate: The smartphone vibrates and makes a vibrating sound but makes no other sound.

    System Sound: The system sound plays. If the ringer is off, the smartphone vibrates.

    Ringtone: Tap a ringtone. A ringtone that you choose plays.

    Mute: No sound plays and the smartphone does not vibrate.

    The settings you apply here apply to all of your Calendar accounts.
