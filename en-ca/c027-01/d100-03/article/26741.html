Maximize battery life

Battery life depends on how you use your phone. You can maximize the life of your battery by following a few easy guidelines:

  • Charge your phone whenever you can. Charge it overnight.
  • Set your screen to turn off automatically after a shorter period of inactivity (learn how).
  • Keep your battery away from direct sunlight and other sources of heat. Temperatures over 113 degrees Fahrenheit (45 degrees Celsius) can permanently reduce the capacity and life span of any lithium-ion battery.
  • As with any mobile phone, if you are in an area with no wireless coverage, your phone continues to search for a signal, which consumes power. Turn off your phone if you are outside a coverage area (learn how).
  • If you live or work in an area of poor coverage, consider purchasing a signal booster (sold separately) approved by Verizon Wireless. A signal booster can bring the signal into a building and enhance it.
  • If you set up an email account in the Email application, set the interval to automatically download email to every two hours or less frequently (learn how).
  • Lower the screen brightness (learn how).
  • Use a Wi-Fi connection to download system updates (learn how) and applications from App Catalog (learn how). Downloads occur faster over Wi-Fi than over a Verizon Wireless data connection, and thus use less battery power.
  • Keep in mind that frequent use of instant messaging (IM) can reduce battery life. Sign out of your IM account when you're not using it (learn how).
  • Shut down the phone completely if you won't be using it for an extended period of time (learn how).
  • Buy an extra battery as a spare for periods of heavy use or long periods when you cannot charge your phone. To purchase batteries that are compatible with your phone, go to palm.com/store and click Accessories for your phone.
