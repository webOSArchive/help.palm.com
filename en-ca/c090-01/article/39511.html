Delete a message

Do one of the following:

  • In the message list: Throw the message off the side of the screen and tap Delete.
  • In an open message: Tap > Delete.
  • To delete more than one message at once: Tap at the bottom of the message list panel > tap the messages you want to delete > > Delete.

Deleting a message moves it to your deleted items folder, so it's still on your device. Depending on your email account, this folder is called Deleted Items, Deleted, Trash, or something else.

To avoid deleting a message accidentally, you can turn the Delete Confirmation preference on (learn how). If you do accidentally delete a message, you can move it back to the original folder (learn how). To remove a message from your device permanently, delete it from the deleted items folder. To delete all messages from the deleted items folder: Menu (upper left) while in the folder > Empty Trash > Empty Trash.
