Switch among slides in a PowerPoint速 slideshow
  • To move to the next or previous slide: Drag up or down on the screen.
  • To jump to a different slide: Tap the currently displayed slide in the upper-right corner of the screen > tap the slide you want to jump to.
  • To view all slides as thumbnails: Tap in the upper-right corner of the screen > tap a thumbnail to open the slide. To dismiss the thumbnails panel, drag all the way to the right.

If the page number and icon are not displayed at the top of the screen, tap the screen to display them.
