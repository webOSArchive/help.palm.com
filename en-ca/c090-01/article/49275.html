Manage Clock
  1. Open Clock 
    > Menu (upper left) > Preferences.
  2. Do any of the following:
    • To turn off alarms when sound is muted: Tap Play Alarm Sound > Mute Alarm Sound.
    • To set how long you can snooze after an alarm goes off: Tap the current time setting in the Snooze field > tap a time.
    • To change the clock display: Tap the current theme > tap a theme.
  3. Tap Done.

To change the time: Open Date & Time (learn how).
