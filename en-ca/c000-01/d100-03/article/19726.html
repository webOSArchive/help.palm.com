Buy an application using your billing account or by entering a promo code
  1. Open App Catalog .
  2. Browse to the app you want > tap the app name > Download for [amount].
  3. If the application uses Location Services, a notification appears. Tap Continue.
  4. If prompted, enter your Palm profile password to authorize the purchase > Continue.
  5. If you don't have a billing account, you are prompted to set one up (learn how).
  6. Do one of the following:
    • To purchase the app using a promo code: Tap Use Promo Code > enter the code > Use Promo Code.
    • To purchase the app using your billing account: Tap Purchase.
  7. To open a downloaded app, tap Tap to launch. The app icon also appears on the first page of the Launcher, so you can open it from there as well.

A promo code offer can be for a specific app, or for any app (up to a certain amount). You need to have a billing account set up in App Catalog to purchase apps with a promo code; however, your account is not charged when you use the code.
