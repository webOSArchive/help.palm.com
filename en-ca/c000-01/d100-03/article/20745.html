Customize messaging notifications

You can set your smartphone to show a notification and/or play a sound when a new message arrives. These settings apply to all your Messaging accounts.

  1. Open Messaging  > Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts.
  2. In Show Notifications, tap On or Off.
  3. When Show Notifications is on, tap Alert > tap any of the following:

    Vibrate: The smartphone vibrates with no other sound.

    System Sound: The system sound plays. If the ringer is off, the smartphone vibrates.

    Ringtone: Tap a ringtone. After you select a ringtone for messages, below Alert, tap Ringtone > tap a ringtone name. To hear the ringtone: Tap  to the right of its name.

    Mute: No sound plays and the smartphone does not vibrate.
