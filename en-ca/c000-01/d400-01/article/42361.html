Delete a message

In a list view: Throw the item off the side of the screen.
In an open message: Tap .

Deleting a message moves it to your deleted items folder, so itâ€™s still on your smartphone. Depending on your email account, this folder is called Deleted Items, Deleted, Trash, or something else.

To avoid deleting a message accidentally, you can turn the Delete Confirmation preference on (learn how). If you accidentally delete a message, you can move it back to the original folder (learn how). To remove a message from your smartphone permanently, delete it from the deleted items folder. To delete all messages from the deleted items folder: Menu (upper left) while in the folder > Empty Trash > Empty Trash.
