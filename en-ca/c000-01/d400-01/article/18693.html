Upload a picture to the web
  1. Tap a thumbnail to display the picture.
  2. If is hidden, tap the screen to display it.
  3. Tap > Upload.
  4. Do one of the following:
    • If your picture web service is already set up on your smartphone, tap the name of the service you want to use.
    • If your picture web service is not yet set up on your smartphone, tap Add An Account > tap the account type. Enter your username and password > Sign In > Create. After you set up your account, return to the picture in full-screen view and repeat steps 2, 3, and 4.
