Answer a Skype call
  1. To answer the call, do one of the following:
    • Tap .
    • If a headset is attached, press the button on the headset.
  2. You can answer the call as a voice call or a video call depending on the following factors:
    • If the other person makes the call as voice only: The call takes place as a voice call by default. You can send outgoing video by tapping . You cannot see incoming video from the other person unless he or she turns video on.
    • If the other person makes the call as a video call and you have the Auto-Accept Video preference turned on: The call takes place as a video call for both incoming and outgoing video.
    • If the other person makes the call as a video call but you have the Auto-Accept Video preference turned off: To answer the call as a video call, tap in the notification. This turns on both incoming and outgoing video. To answer the call as a voice call, tap in the notification.You can still send outgoing video by tapping .
