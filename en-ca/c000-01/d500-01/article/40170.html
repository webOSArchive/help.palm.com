Use the Launcher

The Launcher shows you all the applications and preferences that you have on your device.

To display the Launcher: In Card view, tap . Tap again to dismiss the Launcher.

Scroll up and down to see the top and bottom of a Launcher page.

Swipe left and right to move among Launcher pages. Or tap a page name at the top of the Launcher screen to jump to that page.

You can customize the order of the Launcher icons.
