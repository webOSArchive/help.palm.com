Use Facebook Tablet for the first time

If you have not set up a Facebook account on your device already, you must set one up when you first open the Facebook Tablet app.

  1. Open  Facebook Tablet .
  2. (Optional) Do any of the following:
    • To change how often the Facebook Tablet app checks for new notifications: Tap Frequency > tap a frequency.
    • To turn HP webOS notifications on or off: Tap On or Off. When this setting is turned on, new Facebook notifications appear on the Dashboard.
  3. Tap Add an Account > enter the account information > Sign in.
  4. You are presented with options for which apps should download data from this account. For each app, tap On or Off > Create Account.
  5. Tap Done.
