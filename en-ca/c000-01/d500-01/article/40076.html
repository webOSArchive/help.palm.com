Open a file
  1. Open  Quickoffice .
  2. The first time you open the Quickoffice app, you have the option to set up the app to work with files from an online account (learn how). If you just want to work with files on your device or with an account that's already set up, tap Go.
  3. Tap an account to view files for that account. Tap My TouchPad to view files stored locally on your device.
  4. Tap Show > Documents, PDFs, Presentations, or Spreadsheets, to see a list of just those file types, or tap All files. If you tap a PDF file, it opens in the Adobe Reader application on your device.
  5. Tap the file.
  6. To return to the file list, tap Documents in the upper-left corner of the screen.

You can view a file in an online account that you set up (learn how), a file that you copy from your computer (learn how), or a file that you receive as an email attachment (learn how). You can view files in DOC, DOCX, TXT, XLS, XLSX, PPT, and PPTX formats.
