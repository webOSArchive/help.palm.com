Change your custom message
  1. Open  Messaging .
  2. Do one of the following:
    • If you already have a custom message, tap the current message > enter the new message text.
    • If you're creating a custom message for the first time, tap the status text (Available or Busy) > enter the custom message text.
  3. Tap .

Your custom message is seen by your IM buddies. It appears next to your IM screen name and status. It can contain any text you want.
