Send and receive IM messages

Before You Begin

Make sure that your smartphone is on and that you're inside a coverage area (learn how). BYB Close

  1. Open Messaging .
  2. Do one of the following:
    • In Buddies view, tap a buddy name.
    • In Conversations view, tap an existing conversation.
    • In Conversations or Buddies view, if you tap , you start a new text message. To switch to IM, tap the icon in the upper-right corner of the screen, enter an IM address from your Contacts, and then tap the address.
  3. Enter the message text > . You can use emoticons in your message (learn how).
  4. When the message recipient replies, the reply appears below your message in the conversation. Repeat step 3 to send another message.
  5. (Optional) Do any of the following in a message you receive:
    • To call a number: Tap the number > .
    • To send an email: Tap the email address.
    • To go to a web address: Tap it.
