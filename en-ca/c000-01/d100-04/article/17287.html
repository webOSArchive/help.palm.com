Work with the Text Assist feature

Text Assist, the assisted text entry feature, works on any screen in which you can enter text, including email, text/multimedia, and instant messages; memos; contact and calendar entry screens; and more.

Misspelled word: Auto-correction corrects the word, underlines the correction with gray, and plays a sound.

Unrecognized spelling: Auto-correction underlines the word with orange. Tap the word to display a list of close matches > tap the word you want. If the word is spelled correctly as you typed it, tap the word > tap + to add the word to the dictionary. A notification appears to indicate that the word was added to the dictionary.

Punctuation: Punctuation is added, for example, apostrophes in common contractions. If you type dont, it is corrected to don't.

Capitalization: Auto-capitalization capitalizes the first word of a sentence and other letters that would commonly be capitalized, such as the letter i by itself.

Expanded shortcuts: The shortcut feature expands common shorthand expressions. For example, it replaces u with you and r with are.

To cancel an auto-correction, auto-capitalization, or shortcut expansion, press Backspace .
