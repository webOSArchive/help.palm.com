Erase data and reset your phone

IMPORTANTIMPORTANT:

Perform a manual backup (learn how) of your data before resetting your phone. Important Close

Open Device Info  > Reset Options.

  • To partially erase data from your phone:

    Tap Erase Apps & Data to erase applications you installed and all application settings and data.

    Tap Erase USB Drive to erase personal files stored on your USB drive, including photos, videos, and music files. Erase USB Drive does not delete applications you installed.

  • To erase all data from your phone:

    Tap Full Erase to erase applications you installed, all application settings and data, and all files stored on the USB drive. (Alternatively, you can press and hold Sym  + Option  + power to perform a full erase.)

    After a full erase, sign in to your Palm profile—this restores your Palm profile data to your phone. Sign in to your online accounts to restore that data. Personal files you stored in the USB drive, such as pictures, videos, and music, are not restored. You can also sign in to your Palm profile on a new phone and restore your data to the new phone.

    Tap Full Erase > Secure Full Erase to erase all applications, all application settings and data, and all files stored on the USB drive. Choose this option to fully wipe your phone so that your data cannot be recovered—if, for example, you sell your phone.
