Use Palm mobile hotspot
  1. Open Palm mobile hotspot .
  2. Tap Off to turn on Palm mobile hotspot.

    3. The  icon appears at the bottom of the screen when the mobile hotspot is turned on.

  3. Be sure you are connected to the network of your wireless service provider. If you are connected to a Wi-Fi network, your phone automatically disconnects from that network.
  4. Provided you already created a secure (learn how) or open hotspot (learn how), Wi-Fi devices that are within range of your phone can now locate your phone and connect to it.

    5. If you already set up a device to connect to your secure hotspot, that device remembers the passphrase and connects automatically to Palm mobile hotspot.

  5. When you finish using your phone as a mobile hotspot, turn off Palm mobile hotspot to save battery power.

You can extend your battery time by charging your battery (learn how) while you are using Palm mobile hotspot.
