Check in to a location
  1. Open Facebook  > .
  2. If prompted, tap Allow Once to turn on Location Services.
  3. If prompted, tap Accept.
  4. Do one of the following:
    • To create a new location: Tap > enter a name for the location > (optional) enter a description > Create Place.
    • To select an existing location: Enter the location you want to check in to and tap it, or tap a location in the list of nearby locations.
  5. Tap Check In.
  6. Do any of the following:
    • To let friends know what you are doing: Tap What are you doing? > enter a message.
    • To let friends know who you are with: Tap Tag Friends With You > tap friends > Done.
  7. Tap Check In.
  8. Do any of the following:
    • To see who else is here: Tap Here Now.
    • To browse status updates for friends who are nearby: Tap Activity and look under Recent Activity.
    • To view information about this location: Tap Info.
    • To see your location in Google Maps: Tap the map.

You can use Facebook Places to let friends know where you are and who you are with, connect with friends who are nearby, and more.
