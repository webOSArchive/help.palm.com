Install a certificate from USB Drive storage
  1. Open  Device Info  > Menu (upper left) > Certificate Manager.
  2. Tap  > tap the certificate name.

Certificates are digital documents that are used to authenticate and exchange information on networks. Certificates can be issued for a user, a device, or a service. You can install a certificate that you copied to your phone from your computer (learn how).
