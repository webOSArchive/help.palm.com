Copy a contact to the SIM card
  1. Open Contacts .
  2. Search for the contact (learn how) > tap the contact name.
  3. Menu (upper left) > Copy To SIM Card.
  4. If the contact has more than one phone number, tap the number you want to copy to the SIM card. To copy other numbers, repeat steps 2 and 3, selecting a different number each time.

    The contact info is copied to the SIM card, and a linked contact is created between the source contact and the contact(s) on the SIM card.
