Search for information

With the Just Type feature, you can search for contacts and applications on your smartphone, search for information within an application, or search the web.

  1. Open Card view (learn how) or the Launcher (learn how).
  2. Begin typing the search term.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • To open an application: Tap the icon.
    • To get in touch with someone: Tap a name to display a person's contact info. Tap an email address to begin a new message. Tap a phone number to call the person, or tap to send a text message.
    • To search your company's Global Address List (GAL) for a contact: Tap Global Address Lookup.
    • To search the web: Tap a web search option.
    • To search for email messages containing the term you entered: Tap Email.
    • To search for web bookmarks or pages you visited containing the term you entered: Tap Bookmarks & History.
    • To display a map of a location you entered: Tap Maps.
    • To search HP webOS App Catalog for apps that match the term you entered: Tap HP App Catalog.
    • To create a new calendar event, message, or other item: Tap New [item].

    To search for an application, you can enter either the app name or a keyword--for example, Meetings for Calendar.

    If your search returns no contacts or applications, the web search options are displayed automatically.
