Accounts overview

An online account is a web-based or server-based location, such as Google, Yahoo!, or Exchange, where you keep contacts and/or calendar information, send and receive email and instant messages, and/or post photos. Such web-based or server-based accounts are sometimes talked about as being "in the cloud."

The HP Synergy feature works in the background to get data from an online account onto your smartphone. After you set up an account on your smartphone, you can access data from that account. For some kinds of accounts, you can also add and change data on your smartphone, which is then synchronized to the online account in the cloud.

Use the Accounts application as a central location to set up and manage your online accounts. When you set up an account, you see a list of some common online accounts to pick from. If you use an online account that's not listed, check HP webOS App Catalog to see if you can download an app for that account and use it with your smartphone.
