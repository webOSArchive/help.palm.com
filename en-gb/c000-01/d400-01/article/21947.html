Update your status
  1. Open  Facebook .
  2. On the Facebook News page, do one of the following:
    • Begin typing your status > Update.
    • Tap What's on your mind > enter your status > Update.

To filter your news feed: Tap News Feed > tap the kind of info you want to see.

To get to the News page from any other page, or to get back to the top of the news feed: Tap Facebook at the top of the page.

To use Just Type to update your status: In the Launcher or Card view, start typing your update. Under Quick Actions > More > Update Facebook Status. The Facebook app opens with your message in the update field. Tap Update.
