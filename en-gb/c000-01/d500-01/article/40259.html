Use the Accounts application to edit or delete an online account
  1. Open  Accounts  > tap the account name.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • To change the account display name: Under Account Name, tap the current name to highlight it > enter the new name.
    • To change which applications are associated with the account: For each app listed, tap On or Off.
    • To edit the account information: Tap Change Login Settings > enter the new email address or username and password > Sign In.
    • To delete the account: Tap Remove Account > Remove Account.
