Get directions
  1. Open  Maps .
  2. If prompted, tap Allow Once to turn on Location Services.
  3. Tap .
  4. If required, tap OK to turn on your location.
  5. If you want to start at a location other than your current location, tap Current Location > enter a new starting location or tap a location you viewed recently.
  6. Tap the address field to the right of Current Location > enter a new destination location > tap Enter, or tap a location you viewed recently. To get directions to an address you saved, tap > Saved> tap a location > Directions To Here.
  7. Do any of the following:
    • For driving directions: Tap .
    • For public transport directions: Tap .
    • For walking directions: Tap .
  8. To see the route in writing, tap > tap the transit mode icons at the top of the directions panel on the left to see driving, public transport, or walking routes. Tap to close the directions panel.
  9. Tap Start to see step-by-step directions on the map > tap to move to the next step > tap to go to the previous step.
Note

NOTE: The Maps app cannot determine your location and therefore cannot work properly unless a location service is turned on (learn how).
