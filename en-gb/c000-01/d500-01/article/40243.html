Update a downloaded application from a notification

When an update is available for one or more apps you downloaded, you get a notification. Tap the notification and do one of the following:

  • To display details about the app and the available update: Tap the app name.
  • To update one app: Tap the arrow icon to the right of the app name.
  • To update all apps: Tap Install Updates.

You can also check for app updates manually in the Software Manager app (learn how).
