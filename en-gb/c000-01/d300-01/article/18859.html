View your call history
  1. Open Phone  > .
  2. Do any of the following:
    • Switch the types of calls displayed: Tap All Calls or Missed Calls.
    • Call a number: Tap the number.
    • Listen to a voicemail message: Voicemail messages are identified by the icon in the call history entry. Tap the entry to listen to the message. This feature is available if your smartphone supports visual voicemail.
    • Display other options for the contact or number: Tap the contact  icon or the contact picture to the right of the number.
    • Delete a call history entry: Throw the entry off the side of the screen > Delete.
    • Clear call history: Menu (upper left) > Clear Call History > Clear Call History.
  3. If you displayed additional options for the contact or number, do any of the following:
    • Call an alternate number: Tap the number.
    • Send a text message: Tap .
    • Save the number to Contacts: Tap Add to Contacts.
    • View a contact entry: Tap View Contact.

If a call history entry displays a number next to the contact picture or icon, this indicates that you made and/or received multiple calls in a row to or from that phone number or contact. If you had calls to or from a contact using more than one of that contact's phone numbers, all calls are collapsed into a single entry, and the most recently used number is displayed.
