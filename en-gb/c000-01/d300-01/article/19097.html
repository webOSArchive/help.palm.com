Use visual voicemail

Not all wireless service providers support the visual voicemail feature. Check with your wireless service provider for information.

Open  Phone  > .

To listen to a message: Tap the caller name or number.

Resume playback.
Pause.
Move forward or backward through a message.

To call back the caller: Tap the contact  icon or the contact picture to the right of the caller name or number > tap the number you want to use to call that person.

To send a message to the caller: Tap and hold the list entry >  > create and send a text or multimedia message (learn how).

To forward a voicemail: Tap and hold the list entry > Forward > via Message > create and send a text or multimedia message (learn how).

To view contact details for a caller: Tap the contact icon or the contact picture > View Contact.

To delete a visual voicemail entry: Throw the entry off the side of the screen > Delete.

Visual voicemail displays all your voicemail messages as a list on the screen.
