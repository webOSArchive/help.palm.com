Select a song as a ringtone
  1. Open Sounds & Ringtones .
  2. Tap the displayed ringtone > .
  3. Tap to the right of a song title to preview it. Tap the song title to add it to your list of ringtones.
  4. When the list of ringtones is displayed, tap the song you added to use it as your ringtone.
  5. To set the Ringtone Volume, drag the slider.

You can select a song to play as the ringtone for your phone.

The ringtone you select here applies globally to all incoming calls. You can also set a unique ringtone for a contact (learn how).
