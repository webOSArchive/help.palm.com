Turn all wireless services on/off (airplane mode)

Turn on airplane mode whenever you're in a location where you must turn off all wireless devices. Airplane mode turns off your phone as well as BluetoothÂ® wireless technology Wi-Fi, and VPN. You can still use apps like Calendar and Contacts.

Do one of the following:

  • Open the connection menu > Turn on/off Airplane Mode.
  • Press and hold power > Airplane Mode or Turn off Airplane Mode.

In airplane mode, you can turn the Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or VPN features on individually: Open the connection menu > Bluetooth > Turn on Bluetooth, Wi-Fi > Turn on Wi-Fi, or VPN.

To turn your phone back on, turn Airplane Mode off.
