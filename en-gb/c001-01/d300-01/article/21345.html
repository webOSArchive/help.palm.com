Create a memo
  1. Open  Memos .
  2. Do one of the following:
  • Tap  > enter the memo text.
  • Open Menu (upper left) > Add A Memo > enter the memo text.

To enter bold text: Press and hold the gesture area and press B. What you type is bold until you repeat the gesture.

To enter italic text: Press and hold the gesture area and press I. What you type is italics until you repeat the gesture.

To create a memo using Just Type in the Launcher or Card view: Start typing your memo. Under Quick Actions, tap New Memo. The Memos app opens at a new memo, with the text you entered in the body of the memo.

Memos are saved as you type.
