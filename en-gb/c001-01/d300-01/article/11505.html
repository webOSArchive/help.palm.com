Update or delete a billing account
  1. Open App Catalog  > Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts.
  2. To change the password requirements or receipt email: Tap the current entry under Password Is Required or Send Receipts, and select a new password requirement or enter a new email address. These settings apply to all your billing accounts.
  3. To change specific account settings: Tap the account name.
  4. If prompted, enter your Palm profile password > Continue.
  5. Do one of the following:
    • To change the state associated with your AT&T account: Select the state > Continue.
    • To change credit card account information: Enter or select the new information > Submit.
    • To delete the account information: Tap Remove Account.
