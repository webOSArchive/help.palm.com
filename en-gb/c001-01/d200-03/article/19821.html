Dial from another application
  1. If a phone number appears in an application as an underlined link, tap the number. This displays the dial pad with the number already entered.
  2. Tap  to dial.

If you are having a conversation with a contact in Messaging, tap Text or an IM account name in the upper-right corner opens a list of numbers you have for that contact. Tap next to a number to dial it.
