Use the Data Transfer Assistant (DTA)

The Data Transfer Assistant (DTA) on your phone completes a process that begins on your computer to export your contacts, calendar events, tasks, and memos/notes. To start the part of this export that happens on your computer, go to palm.com/migrate-pre to download the DTA and prepare your data for export to your phone.

We recommend that you use the DTA only once for each data source on your computer. If you use the DTA to transfer data more than once from a data source, you are very likely to get duplicates of your data.

The DTA is not a synchronization application. Changes you make to data after it is on your phone do not show up in the desktop software. If you want to continue to synchronize with desktop software, go to palm.com/sync-solutions to learn about third-party synchronization software (sold separately).

The DTA allows you to select whether to transfer your data into one of your online accounts (such as Exchange or Google) or to your Palm profile account. Not all of your data is necessarily transferred to the online account you select. The DTA tells you what data is transferred, and where, at the time you select the account. At that point, you can change your mind—but not afterwards.

 

  1. Tap the Data ready for transfer notification at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Tap the account you want to transfer your data to.


    To create an online account at this point: Tap Add an Account. To add an account to your phone, you must already have set it up with the provider (with Google, for example).

    IMPORTANTIMPORTANT:

    If you decide to cancel the transfer, you must cancel it before you complete the next step. To cancel the transfer, tap

    Menu (upper left) > Cancel Import > Delete Transferred Data or Don't Delete Data.

     

    Important
  3. Tap Finish Import > OK.


    NOTE: The import happens application by application and can take anywhere from a few minutes to one hour.

  4. When the notification that the data transfer is complete appears, tap Done.

What happens to my data after the transfer? If you transfer data to an online account, changes you make to the data on your phone are synchronized to the account on the server; and vice versa. If you transfer data to your Palm profile account, the data is stored on your phone and backed up to your Palm profile. This data is not available through any online account.

I have a local version of Outlook and Palm® Desktop on my PC. Can I transfer data from both? Yes, you can. If you have identical data stored in both locations, however, you are likely to see duplicate data on your phone.
