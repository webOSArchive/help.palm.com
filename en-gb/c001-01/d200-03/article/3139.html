Watch a video
  1. Open YouTube .
  2. Do one of the following:
    • To search for a video: Tap Search All Videos > enter a search term > Enter .
    • To display the most highly rated videos: Tap Videos > Popular.
    • To display the most frequently viewed videos: Tap Videos > Most Viewed.
    • To display videos you have recently viewed: Tap Videos > History.
  3. When the video you want appears, do one of the following:
    • To watch the video: Tap the video thumbnail.
    • To view video details: Tap the text to the right of the video thumbnail. 

Videos are displayed on your phone in landscape orientation.
