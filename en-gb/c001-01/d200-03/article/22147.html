Add a folder as a favorite
  1. Open  Email .
  2. If the folder you want is not displayed, tap  to the right of the email account name to display the folder.
  3. Tap  to the right of the folder name.

Favorites appear at the top of Account List view.

To remove the folder from favorites: Repeat steps 1 and 2, and tap to the right of the folder name.
