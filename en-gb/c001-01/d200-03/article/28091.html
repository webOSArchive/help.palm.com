Cut, copy, and paste information
  1. Select the text you want to cut or copy (learn how) > Menu (upper left) > Edit > Cut or Copy.
  2. Insert the cursor where you want to paste the text (learn how) > Menu (upper left) > Edit > Paste.

Some apps include a Copy All or Select All feature in the Edit menu commands.

You can also use keyboard shortcuts to cut, copy, and paste:

  1. Select the text or insert the cursor.
  2. Press and hold the gesture area. The lighted bar begins to pulse and glow more brightly.
  3. Press X (cut), C (copy), V (paste), or A (select all, in the apps that support this feature).

A notification appears that the text has been successfully cut or copied. Pressing and holding the gesture area is sometimes called a meta-tap.
