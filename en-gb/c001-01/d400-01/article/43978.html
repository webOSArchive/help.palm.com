View your current location

Do any of the following:

  • If you have Location Services turned on: Tap .
  • If you do not have Location Services turned on: Tap > Launch > Turn On GPS. Press the center of the gesture area > tap the Maps app in Card view. If required, tap Allow Once to allow the Maps app to use your location for this session.

If you are viewing a map of a location far from where you are currently, this repositions the map to show your current location.
