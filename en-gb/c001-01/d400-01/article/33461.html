Use restricted dialing

Before You Begin

Get your PIN2 from AT&T. If you enter your PIN2 incorrectly and your SIM card locks, get your PUK2 from AT&T.

BYB Close

  1. Open Phone  > Menu (upper left) > Preferences & Accounts > Restricted Dialing.
  2. To turn restricted dialing on or off: Tap On or Off.
  3. If you turn restricted dialing on, enter the PIN2 > Next.
  4. To edit the restricted number dialing list: Tap View Restricted Dialing List and do one of the following:
    • To add an entry: Add an entry > enter the PIN2 > enter the name and phone number > Done.
    • To edit an entry: Tap the entry > enter the PIN2 > edit the information > Done.
    • To delete an entry: Swipe the entry off the side of the screen > enter the PIN2 > Delete.

When restricted dialing is turned on, you can make calls to or answer calls from the numbers on your restricted number list only.
