Search for information

You can search for contacts and applications on your phone, or search the web.

  1. Open Card view (learn how) or the Launcher (learn how).
  2. Do one of the following:

     

    • Begin typing the search term.
    • Use Edit > Paste to enter a search term.
  3. Do one of the following:

     

    • To open an application: Tap the icon.
    • To get in touch with someone: Tap a phone number to make a call, an email address to begin a new message, and so on. Tap a name to display a person's contact info.
    • To search your company's Global Address List (GAL) for a contact: Tap Global Address Lookup.
    • To search the web: Tap the search term you entered to open a list of web search options > tap the option.

    To search for an application, you can enter either the app name or a keywordâ€”for example, Meetings for Calendar or Text for Messaging.

    If your search returns no contacts or applications, the web search options are displayed automatically.

    You can also search for entries or files in apps such as Contacts, Memos, Doc View, or PDF View by entering a few words of a file name or memo, or a contact name or initials.
