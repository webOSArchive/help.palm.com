Insert the SIM card
  1. If the USB cable is attached to the phone, disconnect the USB cable.
  2. If a wired headset is attached to the phone, disconnect the headset.
  3. Shut down the phone (learn how).
  4. Press and release the back cover release on the bottom of the phone.
  5. Insert your thumbnail into the hairline opening between the cover and the phone.
  6. Slide your nail all the way around to the top of the phone until you hear the clasps release.
  7. Repeat step 6 on the other side of the phone.
  8. Lift off the back cover.
  9. Remove the battery by pulling the tab that appears at the top of the battery. IMPORTANTIMPORTANT:

    You must remove the battery first whenever you insert or remove the SIM card.

    Important
  10. Insert the SIM card. 
  11. Replace the battery. Make sure to leave the tab showing at the top of the battery.
  12. Position the back cover over the phone and gently press the top two clasps, located at the top corners. Then press the two clasps halfway down either side of the phone.
  13. Slide out the keyboard, and then press the two clasps at the bottom corners.
  14. Press and hold the power button on the upper-right corner of your phone until you see a logo on the screen (approximately five seconds).

If you inserted or removed the SIM card before you removed the battery, your phone cannot connect to the network. To restore the network connection, do the following:

  1. Remove the battery.
  2. Remove the SIM card.
  3. Insert the SIM card and replace the battery.
  4. Be sure your phone is not in airplane mode (learn how).
