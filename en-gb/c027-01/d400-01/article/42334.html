Receive and read email messages
  1. Open  Email .
  2. Tap to display the account folders > tap a folder.
  3. Tap a message to open it.
  4. (Optional) Do any of the following:
    • To call a number: Tap the number > .
    • To send a text message: Tap and hold the number > tap Text.
    • To add a number to Contacts: Tap and hold the number > tap Add to Contacts. Tap Save As New to create a new contact, or tap Add To Existing to add this number to an existing contact.
    • To send an email: Tap the email address > enter your message > .
    • To go to a web address: Tap it.
    • To share a link to a web site: Press and hold Option  > tap the link > Share Link.
    • To copy a link to a web site: Press and hold Option  > tap the link > Copy URL.
    • To copy an email so you can paste it into a memo or other document: Menu (upper left) > Edit > Copy All.
  5. Tap or  to view the previous or next message.

The Email application synchronizes messages anytime you open a mail folder. It also synchronizes messages on a schedule that you can set (learn how). To sync messages when you have a folder already open: Tap .
