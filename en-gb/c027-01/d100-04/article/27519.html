Line up your favorite applications in Quick Launch
  1. Open the Launcher (learn how).
  2. Scroll to the page containing the app icon you want to move into Quick Launch.
  3. Tap and hold the icon.
  4. When the Launcher pages minimize to cards and Quick Launch appears, drag the icon on top of the Quick Launch icon you want to replace. If Quick Launch contains fewer than five icons, simply drag the icon into a blank space on the Quick Launch bar.

Icons in Quick Launch also appear in the Launcher. You can remove any application except Launcher.
