Manage downloaded applications
  1. Open Software Manager .
  2. Tap Apps to manage downloaded apps that appear in the Launcher, or tap Other to manage background apps and services that do not appear in the Launcher.
  3. Tap an app or service name.
  4. On the app details screen, do any of the following.
    • To read reviews: Tap Review App > tap a review.
    • To add a review: Tap Review App > Review.
    • To share info about the app: Tap Review App > Share > Email or Text Message.
    • To report a problem with an application: Tap Report A Problem > Bug > tap a problem type. Enter the problem description > .
    • To delete an application: Tap Delete > Delete.
    • To reinstall a deleted application: Search for the application (learn how) > tap the app name > Download again for free. If you originally paid for the app, you do not need to buy it again.
