Use links on a web page
  • To follow a link to another page: Zoom in on the page (pinch out or double-tap the page) > tap the link.
  • To dial a phone number that appears as a link: Tap the link to display the dial pad with number entered > tap .
  • To open a link in a new browser card: Press and hold Option  > tap the link > tap Open In New Card.
  • To share a link: Press and hold Option  > tap the link > tap Share Link. A new Email message opens with the link attached.
  • To copy a link or URL: Press and hold Option  > tap the link. Tap Copy URL, and then paste it in any app that allows text edits.
