Open the application menu
  1. In an app, do one of the following:
    • Drag down from the upper-left corner of the smartphone onto the screen.
    • Tap the application name in the upper-left corner of the screen.
  2. Tap a menu item to open it.

If a menu item displays a right-pointing arrow , tap the item to open a submenu of options for that item--for example, tap the Edit menu item to open a submenu containing Cut, Copy, and Paste.

A downward-pointing arrow at the bottom of the menu means that more items are available. Scroll the menu to see more items.

A menu item followed by this symbol  plus a letter indicates that a keyboard shortcut is available for that menu item. To use a keyboard shortcut: Press and hold the gesture area; the light in the gesture area pulses as you do this. At the same time press the letter key of the shortcut.

In some apps, like Contacts, you delete info using a menu item.
