Touchscreen - The Launcher

  1. Application name: Displays the name of the currently active application, or the Launcher. In an active application, drag down over or tap the app name to open the application menu.
  2. Page name: Tap a name to jump to a different page.
  3. Launcher: Shows all your applications. Tap an icon to open an app.
  4. Quick Launch: Bar containing up to five icons for the apps you use most, plus an icon to open the Launcher. You can customize the apps that appear in Quick Launch.
  5. System icons: Show the status of wireless service connections, battery strength, and other system functions. Drag down over or tap the icons to open the system menu.
  6. Notification icons: Show new email messages, alarms, and more. Drag down over or tap the icons to view notification details.
