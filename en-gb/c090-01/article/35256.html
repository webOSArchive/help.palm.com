Create and send a text message

Before You Begin

  • Connect your device to a webOS smartphone (learn how).
  • Before you use your device to send or receive text messages, refer to your smartphone's service plan for pricing and availability of text messaging services.
  • Make sure that the smartphone your device is connected to is on and that you're inside a coverage area. See the documentation that came with your smartphone for information about turning wireless services on.

BYB Close

  1. Open  Messaging .
  2. Tap New Conversation > tap the To field > do one of the following to address the message:
    • Enter a contact's first or last name or initials, screen name, phone number, or email address > tap the phone number when it appears.
    • Tap to open your full contact list > tap the contact you want or enter a name or address.
    • Enter a phone number for a recipient who is not a contact.
  3. (Optional) Tap the To field and repeat step 2 to enter additional recipients. Tap the To field to enter additional recipients.
  4. Tap Enter message here > enter the message text > Enter. You can include emoticons in your message (learn how).

You can also start writing a text message from Card view. Tap Just type > start typing a message. Under Quick Actions, tap New Message. The Messaging application opens at a new message, with the text you entered in the body of the message.
